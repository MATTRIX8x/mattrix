AutoQuickTrade — Quick Manual

One-click trade panel for single pairs and currency baskets, with pyramiding, auto-pyramiding and line-triggered pending entries.
IN CHART VERITAS ⚡

01What it is

AutoQuickTrade puts a compact panel on your chart. One click opens or closes a whole position — a single broker pair, or a full MATTRIX basket traded as its real legs. On top it adds rule-based pyramiding (manual or automatic) and pending entries you set with a line on the chart.

02Setup

  • Chart symbol decides the mode. Any broker symbol → PAIR (1 leg). A MATTRIX synthetic chart → basket: #Big8 (7 legs), #RON/#ROFF (8), #FIX (28 — must be enabled with Allow #FIX trade).
  • Baskets need the SyntheticCharts EA to have created the # symbols first. AutoQuickTrade trades the real pairs; the # chart is only the host.
  • Hedging account required — each pyramid layer is its own ticket.
  • AlgoTrading must be ON (terminal toolbar) for any order to go through.

03The panel

Actions

BUYSELL open at market, now.

PEND BUYPEND SELL arm a line-triggered entry.

CLOSE closes the whole pair/basket.

AUTO arms automatic pyramiding (only shown when enabled).

Info lines

  • Lot mode & risk
  • SL / TP basis (pips or ATR)
  • AutoPyr status (step & max layers)
  • P/L — always the money value, in account currency
  • Spread & block reason

04Entering trades

Market: click BUY or SELL. For a basket, every leg opens at once (wide legs may be skipped — see Spread).

Pending (line-triggered):

  • 1st click on PEND BUY/SELL → a cyan dashed line QT_ENTRY appears (labelled P-BUY / P-SELL) and the button shows DRAG & ARM.
  • Drag the line to your level (at least 0.03% away from price).
  • 2nd click → ARMED. The EA fires the moment price reaches the line, from either side. Before the touch you can keep dragging it; after the touch it is locked.
  • Cancel anytime: click the armed button again, or delete the line.
  • While one direction is set up/armed, the opposite PEND button is locked.
Behaviour on touch: if spread is temporarily too wide, the EA keeps retrying (throttled) until it clears — bounded by Pend timeout seconds and the Pend max dist % chase limit. Any other rejection disarms and is logged.

05Lot sizing & stops

  • Fixed lot (LOT_AUTO): every layer = your lot, or a fixed $ / pip. SL/TP in pips.
  • Risk % + ATR (LOT_RISK_ATR): each layer is sized to a % of equity with an ATR-based SL and TP = SL × reward:risk.
Non-forex symbols (crypto, gold, indices): here 1 pip = 10 points and is broker-specific, so a forex-sized pip count is far too small and the broker rejects it (invalid stops). Scale your pip values to the asset and use the panel's spread reading as the yardstick — e.g. on BTCUSD a 5000-pip stop ≈ $500, a 120-pip spread filter ≈ $12. Prefer pips? Fine — just size them to the instrument. Or use Risk % + ATR, which scales itself.

06Pyramiding

Adding to a winning trade, on your terms. Same rules whether you click or AUTO does it:

  • Only add in profit — block adds while the weakest layer is red.
  • Min pips on weakest layer — how far in profit before the next add (0 = off).
  • Move to BE + BE buffer — pull earlier layers to break-even on each add.
  • An added layer is never larger than your first layer (prop-style cap, applied in both lot modes).

Add purely on your own confirmation regardless of profit? Set Min pips = 0 and Only add in profit = false.

07AUTO pyramiding — two switches

AUTO does exactly what a manual add would, automatically. It fires only when both are on:

  • Level 1 — Settings: Enable AUTO Pyramiding (makes the panel button appear).
  • Level 2 — Panel: the green AUTO button (arms it for this session).
Safety reset: AUTO returns to OFF after every restart, profile switch, symbol change and parameter change — you re-arm deliberately. It is preserved only across a pure timeframe change. AUTO is local: it runs only while MT5 and the chart are live.

08Spread filters

SettingEffect
Max spread per pairSkips individual wide legs and opens the rest of the basket.
Max basket spreadSums all legs; if the total is exceeded the whole basket is rejected (0 = off).

On wide-cross baskets (e.g. #GBP) raise the basket limit or set it to 0, and set the per-pair limit to suit your style.

09Magic number — your separation tool

AUTO and pending only manage positions whose magic matches Magic Number (default 1111). Trades from the panel carry it automatically; manual platform orders carry magic 0 and are ignored. Use the magic to keep discretionary and tool-managed trades apart.

10Safety & limits

  • Online-only: AUTO and pending watch in real time — they act only while MT5 + the chart + EA are running with AlgoTrading on.
  • Kill switch: turn AlgoTrading off, or remove the EA, to stop all activity instantly. CLOSE always works.
  • Baskets are correlated: #FIX = 28 legs in one direction is the extreme case — size accordingly.
  • Beta build stops opening new trades after its expiry date (CLOSE stays available).
